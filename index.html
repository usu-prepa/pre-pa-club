USU Pre-PA Club

The Pre-PA Club at Utah State University supports students interested in becoming Physician Assistants. We offer workshops, volunteer opportunities, guest speakers, and networking to help members prepare for PA school and build connections in healthcare.

This website is a resource hub for students applying to PA school. Announcements and events are shared on Instagram and MyUSU.
